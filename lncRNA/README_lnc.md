#LncRNAs analysis
Many long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) are also active in the cell transcriptome. They are not included in repeatmasker annotations tracks, but they can be found in GENCODE specific annotations. <br>
Here we propose a comprehensive pipeline to study the lncRNAs expression in different types of samples.<br>

The pipeline is composed of the same steps as PERREO, but changing some specific arguments specialized in detecting long non-coding features.
